Nucleobase $\subset$ Nucleoside $\subset$ Nucleotide

- Nucleobase (Nitrogenous base):
	Form base pairs and stack themselves to form chains.
	- Adenine
	- Cytosine
	- Guanine
	- Uracil
- Nucleoside
	Nucleobase + five-carbon sugar
- Nucleotide
	Nucleoside + phosphate group
